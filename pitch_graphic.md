Choose a pitch from either of the drop down menus to plot the simulated trajectory with and without spin induced movement. The menu options are updated each day with the pitches with the largest induced vertical break (positive, rise and negative, drop) and horizontal break (glove side, think slider, and arm side, think sinker). 
See the [accompanying post](/pitching/). for a detailed explanation of how I simulate pitch trajectories.

To see how spin rate, spin axis, and spin efficiency affect pitch motion, you can interact with the [pitch editor](/pitch-editor/).
